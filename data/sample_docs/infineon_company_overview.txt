INFINEON TECHNOLOGIES AG - COMPANY OVERVIEW
============================================

About Infineon
--------------
Infineon Technologies AG is a world leader in semiconductor solutions that make life 
easier, safer, and greener. Microelectronics from Infineon are the key to a better 
future. The company was founded in 1999 as a spin-off from Siemens AG.

Headquarters: Neubiberg (Munich), Germany
Founded: April 1, 1999
CEO: Jochen Hanebeck
Employees: Approximately 56,200 worldwide
Revenue: €14.2 billion (Fiscal Year 2023)

Our Mission
-----------
We make life easier, safer, and greener by connecting the real and digital world.

Company Segments
----------------
Infineon operates through four business segments:

1. AUTOMOTIVE (ATV)
   Revenue share: ~44%
   Products: Microcontrollers, power semiconductors, sensors, and radar ICs
   Applications: Electric vehicles, ADAS, body electronics, powertrain
   
2. GREEN INDUSTRIAL POWER (GIP)
   Revenue share: ~20%
   Products: IGBT modules, power MOSFETs, silicon carbide (SiC) devices
   Applications: Renewable energy, industrial drives, power supplies

3. POWER & SENSOR SYSTEMS (PSS)
   Revenue share: ~28%
   Products: Power management ICs, LED drivers, silicon microphones
   Applications: Mobile devices, computing, IoT, lighting

4. CONNECTED SECURE SYSTEMS (CSS)
   Revenue share: ~8%
   Products: Security controllers, authentication ICs, WiFi/Bluetooth
   Applications: Payment cards, government ID, IoT security
