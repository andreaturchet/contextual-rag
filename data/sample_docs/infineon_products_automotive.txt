INFINEON AUTOMOTIVE SEMICONDUCTOR SOLUTIONS
============================================

Electric Vehicle (EV) Solutions
-------------------------------
Infineon is the #1 semiconductor supplier for automotive applications. Our products 
enable the transition to electric mobility.

POWER SEMICONDUCTORS FOR EVs:

1. Silicon Carbide (SiC) MOSFETs
   - CoolSiC™ family
   - Voltage ratings: 650V to 1700V
   - Benefits: 
     * Higher efficiency than silicon (up to 80% lower losses)
     * Higher switching frequency
     * Smaller, lighter systems
   - Applications: Traction inverters, onboard chargers, DC-DC converters

2. IGBT Modules
   - HybridPACK™ family for traction inverters
   - EDT2 technology with optimized chip design
   - Power ratings up to 800kW
   - Used by major EV manufacturers worldwide

3. Gate Drivers
   - EiceDRIVER™ family
   - Galvanically isolated
   - Integrated protection features
   - Essential for controlling power switches safely

ADAS and Autonomous Driving:

1. Radar Sensors
   - RASIC™ family of radar ICs
   - 77GHz automotive radar
   - Applications:
     * Adaptive cruise control
     * Blind spot detection
     * Autonomous emergency braking
     * Parking assistance

2. AURIX™ Microcontrollers
   - 32-bit TriCore™ architecture
   - ASIL-D safety certification
   - Used in critical vehicle functions
   - Applications:
     * Powertrain control
     * Chassis and safety
     * ADAS domain controllers

3. Pressure Sensors
   - XENSIV™ family
   - Applications: Tire pressure monitoring, brake systems
