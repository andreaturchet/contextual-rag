INFINEON SUSTAINABILITY AND ESG REPORT
======================================

Our Commitment
--------------
At Infineon, sustainability is at the core of our business strategy. Our semiconductors 
enable solutions that address global challenges like climate change, resource scarcity, 
and the need for secure digital infrastructure.

Carbon Footprint Reduction
--------------------------
Infineon's products help avoid CO2 emissions that are 27 times greater than the 
emissions generated during their production.

TARGETS:
- Carbon neutral by 2030 (Scope 1 and 2 emissions)
- 70% reduction in CO2 emissions by 2025 (vs. 2019 baseline)
- 100% renewable electricity by 2030

PROGRESS (FY 2023):
- 47% reduction in CO2 emissions achieved
- 78% renewable electricity share
- Joined RE100 initiative

Product Impact
--------------
Our semiconductors enable:

1. ENERGY EFFICIENCY
   - Power electronics reduce energy losses in conversion
   - Estimated CO2 savings: 62 million tons annually
   - Applications: Industrial drives, renewable energy inverters

2. ELECTRIC MOBILITY
   - SiC and IGBT technologies for EVs
   - Increase driving range by up to 10%
   - Estimated CO2 savings: 31 million tons annually

3. RENEWABLE ENERGY
   - Solar inverters with 99% efficiency
   - Wind turbine power converters
   - Energy storage systems

Water Management
----------------
Water is critical for semiconductor manufacturing.

INITIATIVES:
- Ultra-pure water recycling: 95% recycling rate
- Rainwater harvesting at manufacturing sites
- Water-efficient cooling systems

TARGETS:
- 20% reduction in water consumption per wafer by 2030
