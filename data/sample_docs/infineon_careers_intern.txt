Internship Programs
-------------------
Infineon offers various internship opportunities for students:

1. TECHNICAL INTERNSHIPS
   Duration: 3-12 months
   Areas:
   - Hardware development
   - Software engineering
   - Process engineering
   - Quality assurance
   - R&D projects
   
2. THESIS PROJECTS
   Duration: 6 months typically
   Topics:

   - Power electronics
